Our Story

Bangladesh-Born,
Globally Capable

Founded in 2010 in Uttara, Dhaka, Trust-IT (BD) Ltd began as a network services consultancy for local SMEs. Today we are a full-stack technology partner for enterprises, government bodies and startups across Bangladesh and beyond.

Our 200+ certified engineers combine deep local context with global best practices — delivering solutions that work within Bangladesh's unique infrastructure realities while meeting international standards.
